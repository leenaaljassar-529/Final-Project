Welcome!

In this website you will find many ideas and lessons that can hopefully inspire you to create something and start a new hobby.

Crochet

Crochet is a process of creating textiles by using a crochet hook to interlock loops of yarn, thread, or strands of other materials. You can start with simple projects then progress to more complex patterns like making your own doll and many other things.

Embroidery

Embroidery is the craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn. There are many patterns you can use and there are some simple basic stitches that you can use to create many extraordinary pieces.
